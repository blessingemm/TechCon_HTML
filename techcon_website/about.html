History

TechCon was founded in 1996 as a platform for knowledge-sharing and innovation within the power and energy sector. From its humble beginnings in New Orleans, Louisiana, it has grown into a global conference that brings together industry leaders, experts, and professionals to discuss critical challenges and future technologies.

Over the years, TechCon has become a beacon for collaboration, hosting participants from around the world and fostering the exchange of ideas that shape the future of energy systems.

Mission

Our mission at TechCon is to empower the energy sector by fostering innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing. We aim to equip professionals with the tools and insights they need to build a more reliable, efficient, and sustainable energy grid for the future.

Through engaging sessions, hands-on workshops, and expert-led panels, TechCon serves as a catalyst for transformative ideas that shape the future of the industry.

Past Speakers

Dr. Maya Thompson

Dr. Maya Thompson is a leading expert in renewable energy systems and grid optimization. With over 20 years of research and practical experience, she has been instrumental in shaping global energy policies and advocating for sustainable practices in the energy sector.

Mr. James Osei

James Osei is the Chief Technology Officer at GridX Solutions, where he spearheads the development of AI-powered tools for predictive maintenance in the utilities sector. His visionary leadership has helped utilities reduce downtime and improve system reliability globally.

Ms. Aria Patel

Aria Patel is a cybersecurity specialist with a focus on critical infrastructure protection. As the founder of SecureGrid, she has been a strong advocate for robust cybersecurity frameworks in the power and energy industries, ensuring the safe and reliable delivery of services in the digital age.
